Traditions

Kerala is rich in traditions and cultural practices, such as Traditional Dresses of Kerala Women of Kerala wear sarees and blouse. During festivals, they wear set sarees called Kasavu. Men wear 'mundu' which is a long piece of a garment wrapped around their waist in a certain manner.

Dance

Kerala has around 50 forms of dances. Among these Theyyam, Thiruvathirakali, Chakyar Koothu Koodiyattam, and Ottamthullal are some of the prominent dances of Kerala. Kerala is therefore, popular for its two classical dance form ‘Kathakali’ and ‘Mohiniyattam,’ which lure tourists from world-wide

Specialties

Kerala is known as the land of Ayurveda. It offers numerous therapies like Panchakarma, rejuvenation treatments, and massages using traditional methods . Kerala is blessed with a good climate, and an abundance of natural herbs that makes it the best place to enjoy a rejuvenating Ayurvedic therapy session..

Food

Sadya is a traditional Kerala feast served on a banana leaf. It includes an array of vegetarian dishes, such as sambar, avial, and thoran, served with rice and various accompaniments.
